The Software & Application Innovation Lab (SAIL) at Boston University's Hariri Institute for Computing provides professional software consulting, design, and development capacity to support research efforts across Boston University that involve or require custom software, computational, and data-centric components.

SAIL is committed to promoting innovation within the research community and will learn new technologies and techniques as determined by the needs of any given project.

If you think SAIL may be able to help you achieve your research objectives or would like to discuss what resources are available, please fill out a request form or contact Andrei Lapets (lapets@bu.edu), Director of Research Development, directly. If you are seeking other forms of support for your research (including seed funding), you may also want to review the Hariri Research Award program.

SAIL is seeking professional software engineers and student software engineer interns. Please contact Andrei Lapets (lapets@bu.edu) or Frederick Jansen (fjansen@bu.edu) if you are interested applying for a position.
